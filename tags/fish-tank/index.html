Getting a Deal On Varagesale In this aquaponics system, I am using an ancient microwave stand to support the whole system (trying to limit costs in some areas so I can splurge in others), and so I am restricted to a 10 gallon fish tank. Since I am trying to save money building this system, I decided to buy a used tank, and found the perfect sized tank for $10 CAD on Varagesale from a person that lives within 5 km from me!
